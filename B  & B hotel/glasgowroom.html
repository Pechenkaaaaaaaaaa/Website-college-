In a prime location in the South district of Glasgow, S.H Apartments is located 3.4 km from Glasgow Central Station, 3.4 km from Ibrox Stadium and 3.8 km from Hampden Park. This set 4 km from Pollok Country Park, 4.4 km from George Square and 4.5 km from The SSE Hydro. Free WiFi is available throughout the property and House for an Art Lover is 2.8 km away.

Guests can also relax in the garden.

Glasgow Science Centre is 4.7 km from the aparthotel, while Scottish Event Campus Glasgow is 4.9 km away. The nearest airport is Glasgow Airport, 11 km from S.H. Apartments.
