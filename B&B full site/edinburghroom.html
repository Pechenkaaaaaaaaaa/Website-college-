Situated within less than 1 km of Camera Obscura and World of Illusions and a 13-minute walk of The Real Mary King's Close in the centre of Edinburgh, 29 Queen Street Luxury Flats features accommodation with free WiFi and seating area. The property is set 1.4 km from National Museum of Scotland, 1.5 km from Royal Mile and 1.7 km from EICC. The aparthotel has family rooms.

At the aparthotel, every unit comes with a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen and towels. A patio fitted with outdoor furniture are available in some units. All units will provide guests with a dishwasher.

Popular points of interest near the aparthotel include Edinburgh Waverley station, Edinburgh Playhouse and Edinburgh Castle. The nearest airport is Edinburgh Airport, 9 km from 29 Queen Street Luxury Flats.
